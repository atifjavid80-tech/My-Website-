Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the entire process take? +
The entire process from registration to recovery typically takes 45-60 minutes. Actual blood donation takes only 10-15 minutes.
Is blood donation painful? +
You may feel a brief pinch when the needle is inserted, but the donation process itself is generally painless.
How often can I donate blood? +
You can donate whole blood every 56 days (approximately 8 weeks). Platelet donations can be more frequent.
What should I do after donation? +
Drink plenty of fluids, avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours, and eat iron-rich foods. Avoid alcohol for 8 hours.
Can I donate if I have tattoos? +
Yes, if the tattoo was applied at a licensed facility and has fully healed (usually 3-6 months).
